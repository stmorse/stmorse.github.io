I'm an active-duty infantry officer in the U.S. Army. I maintain this website primarily as an outlet for personal and professional research, with a focus on applied math and statistics.

I recently finished a tour as an assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at the U.S. Military Academy (West Point), where I taught multivariable calculus. I did my graduate (master's) program in the Operations Research Center at MIT, where I did research in complex networks with MIT's HuMNet Lab and the Draper Laboratory.
